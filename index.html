How the match was found
  • 1
    Query encoded to a vector
    "The soup was way too salty" → [0.68, 0.78, 0.82, 0.88 …] — a point in high-dimensional space capturing excess sodium + negative experience.
  • 2
    Every stored review has its own vector
    "The broth made my mouth pucker" → [0.71, 0.74, 0.79, 0.85 …] — similar dimensions fire because the meaning is the same, even though the words are completely different.
  • 3
    Distance calculated to every point
    Cosine similarity measures the angle between vectors. A small angle = similar meaning. "Puckering broth" scores 0.82 — close enough to return as a match.
  • 4
    Nearest neighbours returned
    All reviews within the similarity threshold are surfaced. A keyword search would have missed every match except the first — they share no words with the query.
